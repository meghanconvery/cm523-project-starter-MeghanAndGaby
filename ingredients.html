Now that you know what an ideal plate of food looks like for you, here are some examples of what ingredients you can use to personalize your plate.

+

Fruit and Veggies

Why It's Important

Eating vegetables and fruits is one of the best ways to keep your body hydrated, which will help to improve performance. In addition to hydration, fruits and veggies contain many vitamins and minerals that keep your body healthy and strengthen your immune system.

Examples:

Broccoli, brussel sprouts, apples, mango, kale

+

Protein

Why It's Important

Protein is very important to help athletes both fuel before a workout and also recover after. Protein helps to repair and strengthen muscle tissue as well as well as promotes the muscles growth. Protein can also help your blood to more efficiently carry oxygen throughout your body.

Examples:

Meat, Fish, Tofu, Beans, Protein Powder

+

Carbohydrates

Why It's Important

Carbohydrates are the bodies main source of fuel on any given day. They are also the quickest form of energy for athletes to absorb. On days with more intense training, carbohydrates will help an athlete feel more energized and also keep them energized for longer periods of time.

Examples:

Rice, Pasta, Bread, Oatmeal, Bananas, Potatoes
